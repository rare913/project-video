Madara Uchiha

Madara Uchiha is a central antagonist in the "Naruto" series, known for his immense power, strategic brilliance, and complex motivations.

A member of the Uchiha clan, Madara grew up during the Warring States Period, where he became a prodigy alongside his brother Izuna. His deep but ultimately tragic friendship with Hashirama Senju, later the First Hokage, shaped his worldview. Disillusioned by the endless cycle of war, Madara sought to create a world free of suffering through extreme means, ultimately becoming a villain. His ambition and ideology drove him to seek control over the world, leading to major conflicts in the series.
